# 🚀 **Schma – Real-Time Voice-to-Action Infrastructure**

**Problem**
Speech-to-text and LLMs are powerful but messy. Developers who want to turn speech into actions, structured data, or enhanced text spend weeks stitching together APIs, handling context, managing costs, and dealing with compliance. The result: brittle apps, unpredictable bills, and endless debugging.

**Solution**
Schma is the **real-time voice-to-action layer** that sits between STT providers and LLMs. With a single config, developers can:

-   Convert speech into **functions**, **structured outputs**, or **enhanced text**.
-   Switch parsing modes **mid-session** without dropping context.
-   Debug with **full session replay, transcripts, and audit trails**.
-   Trust built-in **PII/PHI redaction, cost transparency, and compliance controls**.

**Why Now**
AI voice interfaces are exploding, but developers lack reliable infra. Just as Twilio became the default for SMS and Stripe became the default for payments, Schma is positioned to become the **default layer for voice-to-action**.

**Current Traction**

-   Fully working MVP with session intelligence, replay debugger, and cost transparency.
-   Early showcase apps (NDIS form filler, test-and-tag compliance, voice notes) built in days to prove flexibility.
-   Initial interest from B2B niches where compliance and accuracy are critical.

**Business Model**

-   **Usage-based API pricing** (per minute of audio processed).
-   **Enterprise add-ons**: BYO database, audio storage, premium compliance.
-   **Developer adoption strategy**: SDKs, snippets, and embeddable components for rapid integration.

**Vision**
Schma will be the **infrastructure layer for voice automation across industries** — powering thousands of apps that need real-time speech-to-action. By providing cost control, compliance, and developer-first tooling, Schma will make building with voice as simple as dropping in Stripe for payments or Twilio for messaging.
