# 📊 **Schma Pitch Deck Outline**

---

## 1. **Problem**

Developers want to turn speech into actions, structured data, or summaries.

-   STT + LLMs are powerful but brittle:

    -   Hard to handle context and partial speech.
    -   Costs are unpredictable.
    -   Compliance (PII/PHI) is an afterthought.

-   Building reliable voice-powered apps today takes weeks → and they still break.

For businesses:

-   Companies are under pressure to make workflows more efficient.
-   Voice-to-action is a natural fit (fast, hands-free, intuitive)
-   Current solutions are siloed apps -- not infrastructure they can embed into their own systems

Net result:

-   Businesses want speech -> action efficiency
-   Develoipers can't build it reliabily or cost-effectively
-   There's no trusted voice-to-action platform that sovles both

## 2. **Opportunity**

-   The market is **B2B and B2D**:

    -   Developers integrate Schma into their company’s apps or internal tools.
    -   Businesses adopt Schma-powered apps to streamline operations (compliance, customer service, productivity).

-   **Macro trend:**

    -   AI adoption is accelerating.
    -   Speech interfaces are becoming expected (think of ChatGPT’s voice mode, Amazon Alexa in enterprise).
    -   But companies need **infrastructure**, not just consumer apps.

**Schma’s Role:**

-   For developers → fastest way to build reliable voice-powered tools.
-   For businesses → efficiency layer that drops straight into existing workflows.

## 3. **Solution**

-   **Schma = Real-Time Voice-to-Action Infrastructure.**
-   Sits between STT and LLMs.
-   With a single config, developers can:

    -   Parse **functions** (strict, schema-based commands).
    -   Generate **structured outputs** (summaries, tasks, CRM fields).
    -   Apply **enhanced speech transforms** (grammar fix, translation, one-off summaries).

-   All with **session intelligence**, **replay debugging**, **compliance controls**, and **real-time cost transparency**.
