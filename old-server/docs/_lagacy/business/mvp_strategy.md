# MVP initial strategy after completion.

## Is building showcase apps first a good strategy?

Low-risk approach, likely smart

-   Tangible validation: building small niche apps (1 week each) proves core tech work across scenarios.
-   Demo-ready stories: You’ll have real use cases to demo — helpful when pitching accelerators, investors, or early adopters.
-   Learning & feedback loop: You gain insights into edge cases, UX, integration, and user feedback before generalizing your core product.
-   Marketing leverage: Instead of abstract “core tech,” you're marketing solved problems with real results.
