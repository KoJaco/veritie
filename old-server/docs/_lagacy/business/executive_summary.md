# 📄 Schma: Executive Summary

**Schma** is a developer-first infrastructure platform that transforms speech into structured actions, insights, and enhanced text — in real time. By sitting between STT providers and LLMs, Schma makes it simple, reliable, and cost-transparent for developers and businesses to integrate voice-to-action capabilities into their products.

### Problem

Businesses increasingly rely on voice data, but current tools fall short:

-   **STT providers** (Deepgram, AssemblyAI, Whisper) deliver raw transcripts only.
-   **Vertical apps** (Otter.ai, Fireflies) don’t integrate into enterprise workflows.
-   **Agent builders** (Vapi, Voiceflow) lack transparency, debugging, and compliance.

Developers face high integration costs, unpredictable pricing, and limited visibility into how speech becomes action.

### Solution

Schma provides the missing **orchestration and developer experience layer**.

-   **Real-time & batch orchestration** across multiple STT/LLM providers.
-   **Structured outputs & function calls** defined by developers.
-   **Session intelligence** to maintain order and merge related calls.
-   **Replay debugger & audit trail** for full visibility into transcripts and actions.
-   **Built-in compliance** with PII/PHI redaction.
-   **Cost transparency & caching** to reduce token spend by 10–20%.
-   **SDKs and snippets** for fast integration.

Schma is the **Stripe/Twilio of voice-to-action** — trusted infra, not a consumer app.

### Market & Model

-   **Target users**: Developers first, expanding to B2B SaaS and enterprise.
-   **Use cases**: Productivity apps, compliance workflows, healthcare, legal, and finance.
-   **Business model**: Usage-based (minutes + tokens), with free trial credits, developer PAYG tier, volume discounts, and enterprise add-ons (BYO database, audio storage, compliance features).
-   **Margins**: Current infra cost ≈ **\$0.009/min**; Schma priced at \$0.05–0.10/min yields 70–80% gross margins while undercutting incumbents.

### Competitive Edge

Schma differentiates with **developer-first design, provider neutrality, compliance-ready features, and unmatched transparency**. No competitor offers session replay, real-time cost estimation, and robust orchestration in one platform.

### Vision

As more businesses embed AI-driven voice features, Schma becomes the default **voice-to-action layer**: provider-agnostic, compliance-first, and trusted by developers to make speech usable, actionable, and affordable at scale.
