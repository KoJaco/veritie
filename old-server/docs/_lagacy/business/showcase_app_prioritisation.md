# 📊 Showcase App Ideas for Schma

---

## 1. **Test-and-Tag (Electrical Compliance App)**

**Summary:** Voice-driven test-and-tag tool for electricians. Switch schemas mid-session to handle different appliance types, populate structured fields with voice, and update UI live.

-   ✅ Strong technical showcase (schema switching, structured outputs, live UI).
-   ✅ Founder-market fit (your personal background adds credibility).
-   ❌ Niche, small market — not a strong standalone business.
-   ❌ Harder to market broadly (limited user base).

**Value to Schma:** Great for showing **technical power** in a focused workflow.

---

## 2. **NDIS Form Filler (Compliance Workflow App)**

**Summary:** Voice-fills structured NDIS compliance forms, exports to PDF, users upload to provider. Saves time for disability support workers.

-   ✅ Market validation (friends report pain, hours/week lost).
-   ✅ Strong compliance use case → shows PHI/PII redaction value.
-   ✅ Government-backed funding → credible enterprise angle.
-   ❌ Narrow scope (NDIS-specific, not generalisable beyond AU).
-   ❌ Export/UX complexity may increase dev time.

**Value to Schma:** Great for **market validation** + **compliance story**.

---

## 3. **“My Day” Productivity Planner (Consumer Productivity App)**

**Summary:** Daily planner app with command-based navigation and structured notes. Voice as primary interface, cleaned/structured notes as output.

-   ✅ Huge market, instantly relatable.
-   ✅ Easy to demo on Product Hunt / HN.
-   ✅ Fun project you’d personally use (fast iteration).
-   ❌ Saturated space, risks being dismissed as “yet another planner.”
-   ❌ Less aligned with Schma’s B2B/B2D positioning.

**Value to Schma:** Best for **marketing buzz**, weakest for business/investor credibility.

---

## 4. **Template Builder + Voice Input (Generic Forms Showcase)**

**Summary:** A domain-agnostic app where users pick/create a template (structured fields), then fill it by voice. Supports schema switching, structured outputs, compliance mode, and export (PDF/JSON). Can serve medical, legal, NDIS, electrical compliance, etc.

-   ✅ Broadly applicable across industries.
-   ✅ Highlights Schma’s **platform flexibility** (infra, not an app).
-   ✅ Great investor story → _“build vertical apps like Heidi in days with Schma.”_
-   ✅ Easy to extend by swapping templates (healthcare, legal, finance).
-   ❌ Slightly more build complexity than #1/#2, since you need a basic template builder UI.

**Value to Schma:** Best balance of **tech showcase, market appeal, and investor narrative**.

---

# 🏆 **Ranking for Schma’s Strategy**

1. **Template Builder + Voice Input (#4)** → Broad applicability, strongest for investors, best “platform” story.
2. **NDIS Form Filler (#2)** → Real market validation + compliance showcase (could piggyback off #4 as a template).
3. **Test-and-Tag (#1)** → Niche but sharp technical demo; great founder-market-fit story.
4. **Daily Planner (#3)** → Useful for marketing buzz/Product Hunt, but weakest strategic value.

---

⚡ **Recommendation:**

-   Start with **#4 (Template Builder)** as your flagship showcase (covers multiple industries at once).
-   Package **#2 (NDIS)** and **#1 (Test-and-Tag)** as _template examples_ inside #4 rather than standalone apps (maximises reuse of your effort).
-   Use **#3 (Daily Planner)** later for buzz/marketing.
